# 🇯🇵 JAPAN ON A BUDGET (Free Ebook)

A free landing page project offering a complete beginner-friendly guide to traveling Japan on a budget.
Perfect for first-time travelers who want to explore Japan for 7 nights.
---

## ✨ About the Project

This project is a simple landing page designed to promote and distribute a free ebook about budget travel in Japan.

The ebook includes:
- 7-night itinerary 🇯🇵
- Absolute Must-Haves items in my baggage ✈️   
- Budget Hotel Options 🏨  
- Tips to Travel Alone in Japan 🚆
- Budget Calculator & Useful Links 💸  

---

## 🎯 Objective

Help young travelers plan their first trip to Japan in a affordable way.
