Where to go? 7-night Japan itinerary

A beginner-friendly route through Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka & more

By Jenne PK · April 2026 · 10 min read

When I started planning my trip to Japan, I had no idea where to go. Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka… everything looked amazing but overwhelming.

So I built a simple route — realistic, balanced, and beginner-friendly. This is the itinerary I’m personally using for my solo trip.

7-night recommended route

  • Nights 1–2: Tokyo → Shinjuku, Akihabara, Harajuku, TeamLab
  • Night 3: Hakone → Onsen, Mt Fuji views
  • Nights 4–5: Kyoto → Fushimi Inari, Gion, Bamboo forest
  • Night 6: Nara → Deer park, temples
  • Night 7: Osaka → Dotonbori, street food night

Why this route works

It’s not about seeing everything — it’s about experiencing Japan step by step: modern life → nature → tradition → food → energy.
